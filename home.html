MISSION

We aim to provide a platform for college students to engage in CIS-related activities and events. Our club offers a range of opportunities for students to advance their skillsets in CIS topics. Whether you are a beginner or and experienced professional, our club welcomes you to be apart of our vibrant community dedicated to CIS education and exploration.

Our upcoming competition will be the Midwest Collegiate Computing Conference that will be held on March 21, 2025. MCW3 is an event held in Grand Rapids that will have many different competitions surrounding technology, including Cybersecurity, Programming, AI, Data Analysis, Java, and etc. No experience necessary.

Our most recent club meeting was held on February 28, 2025. We discussed the MWC3 competition, volunteers needed to develop the website, and future competitions.
